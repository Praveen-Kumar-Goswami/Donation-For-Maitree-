About Donation For Maitree

Donation For Maitree is a volunteer-driven non-profit organization dedicated to providing immediate and effective emergency medical services to the community.

Founded on the principles of compassion and community service, we operate a fleet of ambulances and collaborate with local healthcare providers to ensure that help reaches those who need it most, regardless of their financial situation. Our mission is to build a safety net for our community, one life at a time.
